
# Day 01 – Introduction to DevOps and Cloud

#### What is your understanding of DevOps and Cloud Engineering?

DevOps is a culture and practice introduced in 2009 by Patrick Debois to bridge the gap between development and operations. It promotes collaboration, automation, and continuous delivery to reduce time-to-market and improve reliability. Using tools like Git, Jenkins, Docker, Kubernetes, and monitoring systems, DevOps enables faster releases, better scalability, and fewer production failures.


#### Why you are starting learning DevOps & Cloud?

Building on this experience, I decided to fully transition my career into DevOps, aiming to excel in this field and achieve both professional growth and financial advancement.
